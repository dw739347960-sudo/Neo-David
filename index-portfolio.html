我的书架

My bookshelf

这些书真正改变了我的思考方式。

Books that genuinely changed how I think.

1
高效能人士的7个习惯
The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People
Stephen R. Covey
★★★★★
"以终为始"彻底改变了我做事的方式。每次开始一件事之前，先问自己：我想要的结果到底是什么？
"Begin with the end in mind" completely changed my approach. Before anything, I ask: what result do I actually want?
2
明朝那些事儿
Those Things of the Ming Dynasty
当年明月
Dangnian Mingyue
★★★★★
历史不是年份和事件的堆砌，是真实的人在做选择。在任何处境下，人都有选择的余地。
History isn't dates — it's real people making choices. You always have a choice.
3
不拘一格
No Rules Rules
里德·哈斯廷斯 / 艾琳·迈耶
Reed Hastings / Erin Meyer
★★★★☆
真正的效率不是来自管控，而是来自信任和透明。
Real efficiency comes from trust and transparency, not control.
4
亚马逊编年史
The Everything Store / Amazon Unbound
布拉德·斯通
Brad Stone
★★★★★
长期主义不是口号，是一种可以量化的策略。
Long-termism isn't a slogan — it's a quantifiable strategy.
5
曾国藩传
Biography of Zeng Guofan
张宏杰
Zhang Hongjie
★★★★★
资质平庸不可怕，可怕的是放弃积累。结硬寨，打呆仗。
Mediocre talent isn't scary — giving up on accumulation is. Build strong forts, fight steady battles.

握拍是一切的起点
Grip is everything
正确的握拍比你想象的重要10倍。
Correct grip matters 10x more than you think.
02
脚比手重要
Feet before hands
羽毛球是脚下的运动。步法好了，问题就少了。
Badminton is footwork. Good feet solve most problems.
03
鞭打发力的秘密
The whip secret
不靠蛮力，靠手腕前臂配合。
Wrist-forearm coordination doubles your smash.

$40-70 is enough. Skill matters 100x more.
